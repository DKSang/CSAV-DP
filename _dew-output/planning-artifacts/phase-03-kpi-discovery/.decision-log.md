## Step 1 — KPI Scope Group

**Question:** Which KPI groups should be included in the first KPI Discovery round?

**Decision:** A. Use all four KPI groups in priority order, based on the SPIA Viet Nam Report.

Priority order:

1. **Monitoring KPIs** — identify and rank climate/agricultural risk by location and time.
2. **Explanation signals** — explain which factors contribute to risk, such as drought, salinity, flooding, rainfall, reservoir water level, crop season, planting date, or geographic exposure.
3. **Advisory outputs** — generate evidence-bounded recommendation categories/actions for the farmer-facing web app.
4. **Data quality KPIs** — measure freshness, completeness, traceability, and quality status of data and advisory outputs.

**Interpretation:** KPI Discovery will not invent generic KPIs. It will derive candidate KPI hypotheses from the SPIA Viet Nam Report, especially CS-MAP, adaptation planning, VHLSS measurement logic, province-level agricultural plans, risk levels, and recommended adaptation options.
